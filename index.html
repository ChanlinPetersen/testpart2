ABOUT ME

Technical Game Artist & Developer

With over 2 years of experience in game development, I specialize in creating unique 3D assets in Blender and bringing them to life through animations in Unity. My technical art skills allow me to bridge the gap between artistic vision and functional gameplay.

I'm proficient in modeling, texturing, and rigging 3D characters and environments in Blender, with a strong focus on optimizing assets for real-time rendering. In Unity, I implement these assets with responsive animations and visual effects to create immersive experiences.

On the programming side, I'm comfortable with C# scripting in Unity, enabling me to prototype gameplay mechanics, implement animation state machines, and solve technical challenges. I've participated in numerous game jams, creating everything from 3D shooters to mobile games, which has honed my ability to work across different aspects of game development.

When I'm not creating games, you'll find me playing them - analyzing their mechanics, art direction, and what makes their gameplay compelling.
