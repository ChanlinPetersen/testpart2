RAT:Lost in NYC

About This Game

You play as a scrappy rat fighting for survival in the abandoned streets of New York City. The once-bustling metropolis now lies eerily empty, overgrown with weeds and haunted by feral creatures. As the rat, you must: Scavenge for food and supplies in derelict buildings and trash-filled alleys Evade predators like stray dogs, hawks, and other urban threats Solve environmental puzzles to access new areas Uncover the mystery behind the city's sudden abandonment

My Contributions: Designed and modeled the atmospheric ruined city environment in Blender Implemented dynamic lighting to create haunting urban landscapes in Unity Developed the day/night cycle affecting gameplay and enemy behavior Created interactive objects and physics-based puzzles The game blends survival mechanics with environmental storytelling, challenging players to explore while managing hunger, stamina, and danger. Every shadow could hide either salvation... or your demise. How will you survive the concrete jungle?
